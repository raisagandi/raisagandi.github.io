Hi, I'm Raisa. I live to learn.

I’m an Indonesian polymath working in the USA.

I was created and packaged in Jakarta, Indonesia for the first 18 years of my life.

Then, I was exported to California and repackaged even further. I exposed myself to a breadth of subjects, ranging from STEM to social sciences and the humanities: I learned programming, economics, anthropology, psychology, music, English composition, and the list goes on. I majored in Computer Science and minored in Music at the University of California, Davis, and I completed my college education in a little over three years.

Next, I was shipped to Massachusetts, where I worked at Bose's Alexa Voice Cloud Team. There, I stretched my abilities as an engineer, and I was an apprentice to the most amazing and accomplished mentors who have now become my friends.

I may be repackaged or shipped to other stepping stones in life, but one thing remains certain: I will always remain an apprentice. While I may evolve and move on to other destinations in this world, the relationships that I've built along the way are as permanent as I want them to be.

I'm a software engineer by trade, and an artist by heart.

I've found my medium of self-expression through music.
