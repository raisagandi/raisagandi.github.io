Hi, I'm Raisa. I live to learn.

I’m an Indonesian working in Canada.

I was created and packaged in Jakarta, Indonesia for the first 18 years of my life.

Then, I was exported to Davis, California and repackaged even further. I exposed myself to a breadth of subjects, ranging from STEM to social sciences and the humanities. I majored in Computer Science and minored in Music at the University of California, Davis, and I completed my college education in a little over three years.

Next, I was shipped to Massachusetts, where I worked at Bose's Alexa Voice Cloud Team. There, I stretched my abilities as an engineer, and I was an apprentice to the most amazing and accomplished mentors who have now become my friends.

I continued my adventure as a full-stack software engineer in the transportation industry, making an impact through code and culture. In that role, I discovered my love for relationship-building, something I carry with me to this day.

My next stop was Halifax, Canada, where I attended a master's program in Applied Computer Science at Dalhousie University.

A year later, I moved to Toronto to explore cybersecurity at a financial institution, where I honed my stakeholder management skills and presented to executives. Currently, I work as a software developer at Clio, a legal technology company in Toronto, Canada.

I may be repackaged or shipped to other stepping stones in life, but one thing remains certain: I will always remain an apprentice. While I may evolve and move on to other destinations in this world, the relationships that I've built along the way are as permanent as I want them to be.
